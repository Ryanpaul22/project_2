Best Post-Workout Nutrients

Whey Protein Isolate:

Whey protein isolate is the first essential post workout must-have to aid in maximum recovery. This type of protein provides the necessary amino acids to ignite protein synthesis, protecting your muscles from catabolism (break down), which you're prone to after an intense training session.

Best Post-Workout Nutrients

Creatine HCl

Creatine supplementation has been proven time and time again to be an effective ergogenic aid for athletes. Improving ATP resynthesis allows the working muscles to perform better for longer. In addition to consuming creatine before training, it is wise to consume some after exercise to help replenish what has been utilized.

Best Post-Workout Nutrients

Digestive Support

We've already discussed the importance of the use of easily digestible nutrients in the post-workout window as the gut is compromised due to heightened cortisol levels. For this reason, having an effective digestive aid in your post-workout shake is beneficial to ensure better nutrient assimilation, yielding better recovery and less stomach disturbance.

Best Post-Workout Nutrients

Betaine

Anhydrous betaine has been shown to help improve strength, power, and muscular endurance. It does this in part by promoting better hydration as well as fighting cell stress. Utilizing anhydrous betaine in the post-workout window can not only help with recovery, but it will also prepare your body for the next intense workout.
